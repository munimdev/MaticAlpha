Earn Matic Everyday With MaticAlpha

The contract offers 10% to 112% net interest, if capitals are excluded, within a period of 5 to 35 days

IMPORTANT NOTES
  1. Deposits amount ranges from 10 Matic and above.
  2. Distintive and additional reward is awarded specially to holders (Hold Reward) to strengthen the contract as well as increase its life span.
  3. Given that participants don't withdraw their plan reward prematurely, they will get hold reward. Hold-reward varies with staking plans and are withrawable anytime.
  4. You get your hold reward, wether or not you withdraw it prematurely.
  5. Participants can stake/invest on upto 5 plans simultaneously.
  6. You can withdraw all your balance collectively or separately.
  7. You can be hold-bonus active or innactive for one, more than one or all of your active stake/investment plans.
  8. A reinvestment bonus is instantly paid to your address when you reinvest. Reinvestment bonus varies with staking plans.
